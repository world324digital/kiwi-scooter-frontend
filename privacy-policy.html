General

We collect personal information such as your name, email address when you sign up for our service.

We use this information to provide the eScooter sharing service to you, improve our service, and communicate with you about updates and promotions.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Intellectual Property

We own all intellectual property relating to the Kiwi City eScooter sharing service, including trademarks, logos, and software used in the service.

You may not reproduce or distribute any part of our service without our prior written consent.

Security and storage

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your personal information is stored in secure servers that are protected by firewalls and other industry-standard security measures.

However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Usage Information

We collect usage information such as your device type, IP address, and location when you use our eScooter sharing service.

We use this information to analyze trends, improve our service, and prevent fraud.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance the user experience and enable certain features of our eScooter sharing service.

Cookies are small amounts of data sent to your browser from our server and stored on your computer's hard drive.

You may choose to disable cookies on your browser, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of our service.

IP Address

We collect your IP address when you use our eScooter sharing service.

Your IP address is used to diagnose problems with our server, analyze trends, and administer the service.
